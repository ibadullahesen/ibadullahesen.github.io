Special Provisions for AirMouse Pro (Mobile Application)

Our mobile application, AirMouse Pro, requires specific sensitive permissions to provide its core functionality. We are committed to ensuring your privacy as follows:

1. Camera Access

AirMouse Pro uses the device's camera to track hand gestures in real-time. This is essential for the application to function as a virtual mouse.

  • Data Usage: Camera frames are processed locally on the device in real-time using the MediaPipe framework.
  • Storage: We DO NOT record, store, or transmit any image or video data from your camera.

2. Accessibility Service API

Our app uses the Accessibility Service API to perform system-level actions such as clicking and scrolling on behalf of the user.

  • Purpose: To convert hand gestures (e.g., pinch to click) into actual touch commands on the Android system.
  • Data Collection: We DO NOT collect, read, or share any personal data, passwords, or screen content through the Accessibility Service. It is used strictly to execute mouse actions triggered by the user's hand gestures.

3. Battery and Background Processing

The app requests permission to ignore battery optimizations to ensure the background service remains stable on devices with aggressive power management (e.g., Xiaomi/Redmi devices).
